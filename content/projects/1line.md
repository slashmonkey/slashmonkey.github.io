1Line is a puzzle game that challenges your spatial reasoning. The goal is simple but addictive: connect all the dots on the screen with a single, continuous line without lifting your finger or retracing your path.

## Features

- **Hundreds of Levels**: Puzzles ranging from simple shapes to complex geometric patterns
- **Minimalist Design**: Clean visuals and calming atmosphere
- **Level Editor**: Built-in tools to create and test new level designs
- **Hint System**: Helpful guidance when you get stuck

## Technical Details

Built with C# and Unity.
